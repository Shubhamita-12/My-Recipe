        Chicken Bharta

  Ingredients:-
1. Chicken breast Piece: 250g
2. Onion: 1
3. Ginger: 1 inch
4. Garlic: 4-6 cloves
5. Black pepper: 8-10
6. Turmeric poweder: 1/2 teaspoon
7. Red chilli powder: according to taste
8. Garam masala powder: 1/4 teaspoon
9. Tomamto: chopped 1 cup 
10. Fresh cream: 1/2 cup
11. Butter: 100g
12. Kasuri methi (optional): according to taste
13. Salt: according to taste
14. Water: 1/4 cup

   Method:-
1. Boil the chicken breast Piece
2. Boil the onion, ginger, and garlic in a separate pot
3. After the chicken is boiled, with the help of 2 forks, shred the chicken into small thread-like pieces.
4. Heat a pan and put the butter in it.
5. After the butter has melted, put the the boiled onion, garlic, and ginger paste in it.
6. Stir the paste till oil is visible from it.
7. Now add, turmeric, salt, red chilli powder, and garam masala powder in it.
8. Again stir untill oil is visible from it.
9. Now, add the chopped tomato and stir for 5-6 minutes.
10. Add the shredded chicken. Stir well in low flame for around 10 minutes. Let the oil come out from it.
11. Now add water into it and put the lid on and let it cook for 5 minutes.
12. After 5 minutes, add the black pepper, fresh cream, and kasuri methi in the bharta. Make sure to turn the heat to lowest and immediately turn off the stove after adding the ingredients.
13. The food is now ready to be served.
